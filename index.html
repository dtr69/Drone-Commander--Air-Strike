Privacy Policy for Drone Commander - Air Strike

Last Updated: 12/13/2023

Introduction

Welcome to Drone Commander - Air Strike! This Privacy Policy helps you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us. By installing and using this app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information Collected by Us

We collect the following types of information directly from you:

  • User Settings: Preferences and settings you choose within the app.
  • Game Data: Information related to your gameplay, such as scores, achievements, and in-app purchases.

Information Collected by Third-Parties

Our app integrates Unity Ads and Google Firebase, which collect information as follows:

  • Unity Ads: Collects data on location, personal identifiers, purchase history, app activity, app diagnostics, and device identifiers.
  • Google Firebase: Collects user interactions, device and usage data, location data, and other diagnostic and performance data.

Data Utilization by Third-Parties

While we integrate Unity Ads and Google Firebase for functionality and performance enhancement, these third-party services use the collected data for their purposes. We do not use this data ourselves, focusing instead on enhancing user experience and app functionality. The data is utilized by:

  • Unity Ads: For targeted advertising, analytics, and ad relevance.
  • Google Firebase: For app performance analysis, user interaction analytics, and functionality enhancement.

This utilization is crucial for the operational aspects of these services but does not influence our development strategies or decisions.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Game Functionality: To provide and improve the functionalities of our game.
  • Customer Support: To assist with any inquiries or issues you may have.
  • Analytics: To analyze user engagement and improve our app.

Data Security

All data, including that collected by us, Unity Ads, and Firebase, is encrypted during transit.
